# HypeMachine - THE WHAT

One line: a control panel for building disclosed AI personas ("AI creators")
and running human-approved marketing campaigns through them across social
platforms, with results feeding back to improve future content.

- Persona = durable asset (identity, voice, disclosure, audience, memory).
- Campaign = disposable mission (goal, subject, type, platforms, lifespan).
- The loop: Persona -> Campaign -> Generate -> Approve (human) -> Publish -> Measure -> Learn.

Non-negotiable guardrails (enforced in code): disclosure on every persona,
mandatory human approval, own-channel engagement only, no sockpuppet
coordination, WhatsApp opted-in only, political content limited to civic
mechanics and specific-claim debunks (no advocacy), mission campaigns never
optimize for raw engagement.
